Awesome Efficient LLM

Curated papers and resources on efficient large language models — quantization, pruning, distillation, and more.

Quantization
LLM.int8(): 8-bit Matrix Multiplication for Transformers at Scale
Dettmers et al. · 2022
Enables 8-bit quantization for LLMs with near-lossless performance using mixed-precision decomposition.
Quantization
GPTQ: Accurate Post-Training Quantization for Generative Pre-trained Transformers
Frantar et al. · 2022
Achieves 3-4 bit quantization of GPT models with minimal accuracy loss using a one-shot quantization method.
Pruning
SparseGPT: Massive Language Models Can be Accurately Pruned in One Shot
Frantar & Alistarh · 2023
Prunes 50-60% of weights from GPT models in one shot without retraining, retaining strong performance.
Distillation
DistilBERT: a distilled version of BERT
Sanh et al. · 2019
60% smaller and 40% faster than BERT while retaining 97% of its language understanding capabilities.
Inference
FlashAttention: Fast and Memory-Efficient Exact Attention with IO-Awareness
Dao et al. · 2022
IO-aware exact attention algorithm that is 2-4x faster and uses up to 10x less memory than standard attention.
Architecture
MoE-Mamba: Efficient Selective State Space Models with Mixture of Experts
Pióro et al. · 2024
Combines Mamba state space models with Mixture of Experts to achieve efficient scaling of language models.
Survey
Efficient Large Language Models: A Survey
Wan et al. · 2023
Comprehensive survey covering model-centric and data-centric efficiency techniques for LLMs.
